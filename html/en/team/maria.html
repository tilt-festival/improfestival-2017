Maria Jõgi

Maria likes to try new things. So it is only logical that next to having a thousand different hobbies, she finally ended up doing improv.

After trying it once, she was hooked. Having participated in Tilt for two years as a performer and an audience member, she could not sit still any more and wanted to be a part of the magic of organising the festival. Maria has now been a part of the festival team for two years.
